How It Works

1. Generate a Code

Open the PINchain app and get a one-time 6-digit code linked to your wallet.

2. Sign & Send

Sign a transaction in MetaMask. Your funds are routed to our smart contract.

3. Merchant Gets Paid

Our backend notifies the off-ramp (like MoonPay), and the merchant gets fiat instantly.

Why PINchain?

PINchain is non-custodial, instant, and globally accessible. Your funds stay in your control. We handle the routing—off-ramp partners handle the rest.
